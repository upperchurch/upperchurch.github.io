Upper Church @ Summerhall

The Upper Church is an exceptionally large performance space hidden away in a corner of Edinburgh’s vibrant Summerhall complex. It is 13.2 m x 12 m and 5.3 m high and is programmed and controlled by the Rose Bruford College, acting as their northern home.

As an extraordinarily flexible space it excels in both seated and promenade forms. The open ceiling enhances the acoustic and allows for a diverse range of layouts. The installed standard rig is always available to hire, whilst additional lighting, heating, sound and video can be provided at the visitor’s request and expense.
